I am a studying Mechatronics at Ontario Tech University

Hey!

I'm Will, and I am working towards a Mechatronics deree and a Professional Engineer's Licence. I have skills and Experience in areas including team managment, computer science, Photography, graphic design, manufacturing, and rapid prototyping

I have been working on volunteer organizations since I was 13. My first position was as technical lead for a fundraising group within a small community organization called the Blenhiem Youth Centre. I later chaired the organization for a year and a half before the centre closed briefly in 2020. I was deeply involved within my Highschool community for all four years, including as Pit Crew Co-Chief for the racecar team, Student Trustee for the 2020-21 school year, and as Student Council President for the 2021-22 year. I worked to promote an inclusive, productive enviroment that was both welcoming and effective.
