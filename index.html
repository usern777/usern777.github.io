Hey!

Hobbies and Spare Time

Here's what I do in my spare time

Hobby

Graphic Design and Digital Art

I have been using Blender for about 6 years, and I have recently started learning the Affinity suite of softwares. I enjoy creating small virtual enviroments, as well as trying out beta features and playing with new techniques. It has come in handy the odd time, like when I needed to show off a cabinet concept in my grade 12 Woodshop course.

Hobby

Cooking

I started cooking because I was tired of the food availible at the caffetiera, and have since discovered I quite enjoy it. I now cook every sunday for a small group of friends, and its a great way to reduce stress.

Hobby

Linux

I first started learning linux with a raspberry pi model B given to me by a family friend, and now run it whenever I can. It is the most versitile software tool I have, and currently have it managing my 3D printer, handling file backups for all of my devices, and I use it on my laptop for notes and general productivity.

Hobby

Microelectronics and Fabrication

I have always been facinated by the process of making things. By combining basic woodworking, 3D printing, and a fair knowledge of several microprocessor systems, I am able to build most a lot of very interesting things. I once connected an old kettle to Siri with a couple of MOSFETs and a Pi 0W (which was really fun until it shot boiling water across my desk).
